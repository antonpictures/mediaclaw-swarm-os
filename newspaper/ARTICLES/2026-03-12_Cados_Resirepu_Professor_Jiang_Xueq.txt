Alice’s investigation concludes that while Jiang’s scenarios contain kernels of geopolitical tension—particularly the volatile Israel‑Iran dynamic and the growing role of autonomous weapons—their assembly into a deterministic timeline leans more toward alarmist storytelling than credible forecasting. Responsible discourse must separate legitimate strategic analysis from sensationalist narratives that feed fear and fringe mythologies, lest the public be led down a path of paranoia rather than preparedness.
